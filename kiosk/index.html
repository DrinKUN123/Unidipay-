How ordering works

  1. Tap "Tap to Start" to open the kiosk.
  2. Select a category (Meals, Drinks, Snacks, Desserts) to view its items.
  3. Tap "Add" on any item you want; you can switch categories and keep adding.
  4. Open the cart (top right) to review your picks, change quantities, or remove items.
  5. Choose your order type: Dine In (eat here) or Take Out (to-go packaging).
  6. Verify prices and totals in the cart; adjust items until the total is correct.
  7. Tap "Proceed to Payment" to continue.
  8. Tap your UniDiPay card on the reader or enter your NFC Card ID; make sure your balance covers the total.
  9. Wait for "Processing Payment" and then "Order Successful"—your order number appears on screen.
  10. Keep the order number for pickup; the kiosk will auto-reset after the countdown.
